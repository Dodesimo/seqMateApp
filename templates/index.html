Automated Pipeline Flowchart:

Our product allows any researcher to almost fully automate their RNA-Seq workflow. Using this product, you can input the raw output of any transcriptome sequencing experiments. The pipeline then returns a complete write-up of differential expression analysis, including automatically generated BAM & SAM files, count matrices, a full-page workup on any differentially expressed genes (with cutoffs designated by the user), and various graphs and charts that visualize the textual and numerical output. Please see the flowchart on the right for a graphical representation of the different automated steps through our LLM-based technology.
